# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: June 17, 2026**

## Overview

Visa Countdown (hereinafter referred to as "the App") respects and protects user privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

## Information Collection

The App **does not collect** any personal information. All data is stored locally on your device and is not uploaded to any server.

### Locally Stored Data

The App stores the following data on your device:

- Visa record information (visa type, expiration date, notes, etc.)
- App settings preferences (language, notification settings, etc.)

This data is used solely to provide visa expiration reminder functionality within the App.

## Data Usage

We use locally stored data only for the following purposes:

1. Calculate and display visa validity countdown
2. Send local notification reminders when visas are about to expire
3. Save your app settings preferences

## Data Sharing

The App **will not** share, sell, or transmit your personal information to any third party.

## Notification Permissions

The App will request permission to send local notifications to remind you when your visa is about to expire. Notification data is processed only on your device and is not sent to external servers.

## Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, data security depends on your device's security settings. We recommend that you:

- Set a password or biometric protection for your device
- Regularly back up your device data

## Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 years of age, and we do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If there are significant changes, we will notify you within the App.
