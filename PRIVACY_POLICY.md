# Privacy Policy

**TrailMaster** ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

**Last Updated:** June 10, 2026

---

## 1. Information We Collect

TrailMaster is a **standalone offline application**. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

### Data Stored Locally on Your Device:
- **User preferences** (app settings, theme choices)
- **Usage data** (offline activity logs, saved trails/routes)
- **Cache files** (to improve app performance)

### Data We Do NOT Collect:
- ❌ Personal identification (name, email, phone number)
- ❌ Location history (real-time GPS tracking)
- ❌ Device identifiers (IDFA, IDFV)
- ❌ Usage analytics or crash reports sent to servers
- ❌ Any data transmitted over the internet

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

All data collected is **stored locally on your device** and used solely to:
- Provide core app functionality (save/load your outdoor activities)
- Remember your preferences between sessions
- Enable offline access to your data

We do **not** use your data for:
- Advertising purposes
- Selling to third parties
- Analytics tracking
- Any form of data sharing

---

## 3. Data Storage & Security

- All data is stored **locally on your device**
- No data is transmitted to external servers
- No third-party services have access to your data
- You can delete all app data by uninstalling the app from your device

---

## 4. Third-Party Services

TrailMaster does **not** integrate with any third-party services, SDKs, or analytics platforms.

---

## 5. Children's Privacy

Our application is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

---

## 6. Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device:
- ✅ You can view your data within the app
- ✅ You can edit or delete your data at any time
- ✅ You can export your data (if supported by the app)
- ✅ You can uninstall the app to remove all data completely
