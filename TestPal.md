# TestPal Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** July 16, 2026  
**Last Updated:** July 16, 2026

Thank you for using TestPal (hereinafter referred to as "the App"). This Privacy Policy is designed to inform you about how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use the App. Please read this policy carefully before using the App.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide Voluntarily

This App is a utility app, and the current version **does not require you to register an account**. Therefore, we do not collect your name, phone number, email address, or other personally identifiable information. We only access the system clipboard when you voluntarily copy the contact email through the "Feedback" feature, but we do not read or upload any other content from your clipboard.

### 1.2 Information We Collect Automatically

To provide features such as device detection, network status, and AR measurement, the App may read the following device information:

- **Device Information**: Device model, system version, screen resolution, battery status, storage space, and sensor data (accelerometer, gyroscope, magnetometer, barometer, etc.), used to run device detection features.
- **Network Information**: Network connection type (Wi-Fi / cellular / no network), local IP address, public IP address, and network speed, used for the "Network Status" feature.
- **Camera and Sensor Data**: When using features such as Magnifier and AR Measurement, the App needs access to the camera, LiDAR, depth sensor, and motion sensors to provide real-time preview and measurement results.
- **Audio Data**: When using microphone test, noise detection, and sound wave dust/drainage removal features, the App needs access to the microphone or audio playback, but we **do not record, store, or upload** your voice content.
- **Location Information**: Features such as Compass and Altimeter may require access to location information to provide more accurate data, but we **do not continuously track** your location, nor do we upload location information to our servers.

### 1.3 Information We Do Not Collect

- We do not collect your contacts, text messages, photos, videos, browsing history, or other sensitive personal information.
- We do not track your behavior in third-party apps or websites through this App.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We collect the above information solely for the following purposes:

1. To implement various utility features within the App (such as device detection, AR measurement, network speed test, compass, etc.).
2. To display measurement results, device status, and network status locally. All core calculations are performed locally on your device.
3. To diagnose and fix technical issues in the operation of the App (only when you actively provide feedback).

## 3. Information Storage and Protection

- **Local Processing**: Core functional data of the App (such as measurement results, device information, and network speed) is processed on your device and will not be uploaded to our servers.
- **Cache Cleanup**: You can manually delete temporary files generated by the App through "Settings → Clear Cache".
- **Security Measures**: We adopt industry-standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure.

## 4. Third-Party Services

The App may use the following types of third-party services:

- **Public IP Query Services**: To display your public IP address, we may call public IP query APIs (such as `https://api.ipify.org` or similar services). These APIs only return your IP address and do not transmit your personally identifiable information.
- **Apple Services**: The App uses frameworks provided by Apple (such as ARKit, CoreMotion, CoreLocation, Network, etc.). Related data processing is subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

Except for the above circumstances, we will not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

## 5. Your Rights

You have the following rights:

1. **Access and Correction**: You can view device information, network status, and measurement results within the App.
2. **Data Deletion**: You can delete all local data stored by the App by uninstalling the App or clearing the cache.
3. **Withdraw Authorization**: You can revoke the App's access to camera, microphone, location, and other permissions at any time in iOS "Settings → Privacy & Security".

## 6. Children's Privacy

This App is not intended for children under the age of 13. If we discover that we have inadvertently collected personal information from a child, we will delete the relevant information immediately.

## 7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated policy will be published within the App or on the relevant page and will take effect from the date of publication. Please check it regularly for the latest content.
